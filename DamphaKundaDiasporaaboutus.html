Dampha Kunda Diaspora Organisation

Dampha kunda Diaspora organisation is a non-profit and social services association, serving the interest of the communities free of charge in the Gambia, located in Dampha Kunda Village. Our aims and objectives are focus on social development, improving the well-being of every individual in the society so they can reach their full potential in life set out in our mission statement , through provision of basic necessity of life in the community to everyone such as building schools, hospital, road and communication ,mosques, constructed bridges and flood barriers , re-surfacing feeder roads, and provide water supply by digging boreholes power by solar panel installation etc.

These are some of the highlights of the projects mentioned above in collaboration with Village Development Committee VDC members to achieve these goals driven by our ambitious plan to bring change to our community. We realise that we cannot do this on our own but to reach out to likeminded people, governments, organisations, and individuals who believe in our causes that impact social empowerment strategies to improve life ordinary citizen in the Gambia.

One of our top agenda is to build a Health centre within the reach of the communities in our village and its surroundings villages. This project was initiated by Dampha kunda diaspora Organisation DKDO and construction project began in April 2022 with an estimated budget of D4,000,000.00 which is funded by its members yearly contributions, individual donor, other organisation, and sponsors. Phases completed into these projects are fencing the perimeter of the land donated by a member of the organisation, erection of main building complex (brickwork) Roofing and plastering.

However, more work still needs to be done to finish, including plumbing, electrical installations, tiling, fixtures, fittings, and office furniture, IT equipment such as computers, laptops, printers, scanners etc. Hospital equipment such as beds, baby monitors and incubators, etc. anything that could enhance Healthcare facility to operate fully and functional health Centre for everyone at the point of need.
