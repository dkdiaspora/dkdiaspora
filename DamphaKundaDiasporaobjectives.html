Dampha Kunda Diaspora Objectives

These are generally consider to be our short-term policies, measures, actions and specific targets that are based on a precise time frame. Our objectives are specific steps and actions that we must take in order to achieve our overall strategic goals. They tell us what we must do and when so as to accomplish a specific objective within a particular period usually months or years. Below is the outline of Dampha Kunda Diaspora’s objectives:

To support our financial aims, the group has unanimously agreed to D11, 000 yearly contribution by each member.

To maintain regular and effective communication among members via our WhatsApp group channel or any social media platforms.

To organise, promote and encourage group gathering of all members either on social media group calls and meetings at a special venue.

Encourage members to inform the group about their upcoming events and functions.

To have all the natives of Dampha Kunda who are based Diaspora joined the group by the end of year 2026.
