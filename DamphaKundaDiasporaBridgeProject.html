Dampha Kunda Diaspora Bridge Project

The Bridge connecting Dampha Kunda to Basse was badly damaged to the extent that It became unsafe for pedestrians, Donkey chart, and motor vehicles to use the bridge. The damaged bridge had caused numerous accidents resulting in serious injuries. The order was passed to ceased using the as it was unsafe and unfit for purpose.

This resulted in local community of Dampha Kunda and the surrounding villages taking alternative route which was about 15KM longer to reach Basse the main trading hub for the region.

Subsequently, Dampha Kunda Diaspora Organisation stepped in by organising a fund raising campaign, the fund was raised and the bridge construction commenced. The bridge construction process took Dampha Kunda Diaspora Organisation months to complete.

The newly constructed bride was inaugurated by Dampha Kunda Village community and the surrounding communities.
