Dampha Kunda United Diaspora Overview

Dampha Diaspora was officially established in 06th August 2018 by members of Dampha Diaspora committee around the world. It is a non-profit organisation with a long term vision to render aids to residents of Dampha Kunda Village, and the surrounding Communities Upper River Region, The Gambia, West Africa.

The group membership is not limited to only the native of Dampha Kunda but to anyone who has a profound interest in the group and its activities is highly welcome to join the group.

Since its establishment, Dampha Kunda Diaspora Organisation has been engaging in various non-profit humanitarian projects such as: building hospital, repairing bridges, roads, and managing maintenance of Dampha Kunda Village water supply system. Currently, Dampha Kunda Diaspora Organisation is at the finishing phrase of the hospital construction.

The organisation is seeking helps from government, individual philanthropist, and other charitable organisations to help complete the hospital so Dampha Kunda Village and its surrounding villages can receive world class hospital treatment facilities.
