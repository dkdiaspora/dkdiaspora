GOALS OF DAMPHA KUNDA DIASPORA

These comprises of our long-term plans, aims and objectives of which we hope to achieve over the next coming decades. They define our purpose of being, visions and set out our mission’s statement.

The Goals of Dampha Diaspora are as follows:

To build a better hospital with better fertilities to serve the Dampha Kunda village and the surrounding Communities.

Reduce maternaty related death by 90%

Eradicate malaria within Dampha Kunda and its surrounding communities

Providing physical and financial support to members who are facing serious crisis in life through no fault of their own such a jobs lost, homelessness and trouble with law enforcement.

To promote unity, togetherness, selflessness, transparency and trustworthiness among all members.

Promoting our cultures, traditions, values and beliefs for generations to come.
