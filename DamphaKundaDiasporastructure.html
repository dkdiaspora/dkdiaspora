Dampha Kunda Diaspora Organisational Structure

In order for an organisation to achieve its overall corporate goals and objectives, there should be in place an effective board committee who set the directions, monitor and manage the affairs of an organisation. The directors or board committee are elected by the shareholders of an organisation.
